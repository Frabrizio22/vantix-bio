## Article 2: Batch COAs

### What I changed:

**Title:**
- ❌ Before: "Why Batch-Level COAs Matter (And Why Most Vendors Don't Provide Them)"
- ✅ After: "Why Most Research Peptide COAs Are Basically Lies"

**Hook:**
- ❌ Before: "You're shopping for research peptides. The vendor shows you a Certificate of Analysis..."
- ✅ After: "That COA is from February. It's now June. Nobody tested YOUR batch."

**Voice:**
- ❌ Before: Polite explanation of industry practices
- ✅ After: Blunt callout of bullshit + why it matters

**Specific changes:**
- "The 'One COA Forever' Trick" section - tells the story of how vendors game the system
- "The Part Where I Show You Math" - real cost breakdown without corporate-speak
- "Why This Matters More Than You Think" - actual consequences, not theoretical
- "The Vendor's Defense (And Why It's BS)" - addresses objections head-on

**Added real talk:**
- "This isn't a scam. It's just how most vendors operate. And it's a massive problem nobody talks about."
- "Maybe. But if Pfizer doesn't trust consistency batch-to-batch, why should you?"
- "Or you can just pay a few extra bucks for actual verified testing. Your call."

**Still compliant:**
- RUO disclaimers
- No therapeutic claims
- Focused on research quality control
- But sounds like a human wrote it
