## COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE (Market Reality Check)

### What Janoshik Actually Offers:
**Janoshik DOES have a verification portal at janoshik.com:**
- Public COA database searchable by batch
- PDF COAs with chromatograms
- Used by multiple peptide vendors
- Based in Switzerland (Europe)
- ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accredited

**Where Vantix Differs from Janoshik's Portal:**
1. **Vantix portal is INTERACTIVE** - Canvas-rendered chromatograms with flashlight effect, tooltips, zoom
2. **Forensic security features** - Watermarking, session tracking, print-blocking
3. **Vantix-branded experience** - Custom UI, not generic lab portal
4. **8-Step Matrix Protocol** - Blind entry, quarantine, digital mirroring (Vantix-specific process)
5. **U.S.-based positioning** - While Janoshik is Swiss, Vantix emphasizes ISO standards
6. **Split-screen visualization** - Dual chromatogram views in one interface

**CRITICAL INSIGHT:** 
The differentiation ISN'T "we test and they don't" - it's "we built a forensic-grade verification SYSTEM around testing, not just a lab report lookup."

### Competitor Analysis:

**Budget Tier ($20-40/product):**
- Amino Asylum, SwissChems, Peptide Source
- Minimal or no testing
- Vague quality claims
- Discount code culture

**Mid-Tier ($40-60/product):**
- Core Peptides, Biotech Peptides
- Some testing (often Janoshik)
- Standard vendor portals
- Influencer affiliate programs

**Premium Tier ($60-100+/product):**
- Peptide Sciences (SHUT DOWN March 6, 2026)
- Limitless Peptides
- High prices, premium positioning
- Variable testing transparency

**Vantix Positioning:**
"Premium quality at mid-tier pricing" - ISO testing + forensic verification at post-discount market rates
