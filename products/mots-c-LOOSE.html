Research Applications

MOTS-C is a 16-amino-acid mitochondrial-derived peptide (MDP) encoded by the mitochondrial 12S rRNA gene. It functions as a retrograde signaling molecule, coordinating mitochondrial-nuclear communication to optimize metabolic homeostasis.

Research indicates MOTS-C activates AMPK-dependent pathways, enhancing glucose metabolism, insulin sensitivity, and mitochondrial biogenesis. Studies explore its potential in metabolic disorders, aging research, and exercise physiology.
