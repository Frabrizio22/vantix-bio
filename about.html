Our Mission

We test what we sell, publish the results, and stand behind the data. Third-party verification should be standard practice, not a premium feature.

Every batch is independently tested by Janoshik Analytical in Prague, Czech Republic. We publish complete certificates of analysis — HPLC-DAD purity, LC-MS/MS identity confirmation, and LAL endotoxin screening — before any product is released.

Why Janoshik?

Janoshik Analytical is the recognized standard for peptide verification in the research community. Their triple-method testing protocol (HPLC-DAD, LC-MS/MS, LAL) provides the analytical scrutiny serious researchers expect.

We chose Janoshik because their reputation is built on accuracy, not vendor relationships. Independent labs that prioritize data integrity over customer satisfaction are rare. Janoshik is one of them.

What We Don't Do

We don't sell manufacturer claims. Every product enters our system with zero assumptions. No COAs, no purity data, no trust. We verify first, label second.

We don't cherry-pick batches. Samples are randomly selected from sealed production runs. What we test is what you receive.

We don't hide results. Complete COAs are published to our verification portal before any batch is released. Chromatograms, mass spectra, endotoxin data — all public, no login required.
