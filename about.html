Our Mission

We test what we sell, publish the results, and stand behind the data. Third-party verification should be standard practice, not a premium feature.

Every batch is independently tested by Janoshik. We publish complete certificates of analysis — HPLC purity, LC-MS/MS identity confirmation, and LAL endotoxin screening — before release.

Why Janoshik?

Janoshik is a recognized standard for peptide verification in the research community. Their multi-method testing protocol provides the analytical rigor researchers rely on.

We chose Janoshik for their established reputation in analytical accuracy and independence from vendor influence.

What We Don't Do

We don't sell manufacturer claims.
Every product enters our system with zero assumptions. No COAs, no purity data, no trust. We verify first, label second.

We don't cherry-pick batches.
Samples are randomly selected from sealed production runs. What we test is representative of what you receive.

We don't hide results.
Complete COAs are published to our verification portal before batch release. HPLC chromatograms, mass spectra, and endotoxin data — all public, no login required.
