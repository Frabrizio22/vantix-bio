Testing Methodology

Every Vantix Bio peptide undergoes rigorous dual-method verification:

High-Performance Liquid Chromatography with Diode Array Detection for precise purity quantification and impurity profiling.

LC-MS Verification

Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry for molecular weight confirmation and identity verification at the molecular level.

Why Verification Matters

Research-grade peptides occupy a regulatory gray zone. Most vendors source from the same Chinese manufacturers, relying on supplier certificates that are rarely verified independently.

This creates risk: mislabeled products, contamination, incorrect concentrations, or degraded compounds that compromise research outcomes.

Vantix Bio eliminates this uncertainty through independent third-party verification. We test what we sell. We publish the results. We stand behind the data.

Our Standards

Purity threshold: ≥98% by HPLC or the product is not listed.

Identity confirmation: Molecular weight must match theoretical mass within ±0.5 Da.

Sterility & endotoxin testing: Available for select batches to support advanced research applications.

Batch traceability: Every vial linked to a specific production batch with full analytical documentation.
