Why Vantix Bio Exists

The research peptide industry operates in a regulatory gray zone. Most vendors source from the same Chinese manufacturers and rely on supplier certificates that are rarely verified independently.

This creates systemic risk: mislabeled products, contamination, incorrect concentrations, or degraded compounds that compromise research outcomes and waste resources.

Vantix Bio was founded after observing how frequently supplier certificates were accepted without independent verification. Our goal is simple: bring laboratory-level verification standards to the research peptide market.

"No assumptions. No blind trust. Just data."

We test what we sell. We publish the results. We stand behind the data.

How We Choose Product Sizes

Most peptide vendors sell whatever sizes their Chinese suppliers offer—typically 5mg or 10mg, chosen for manufacturing convenience rather than research practicality.

Vantix Bio takes a different approach: protocol-first sizing. We analyzed the most common research protocols in published literature and sized our vials to match actual usage patterns, not supplier catalogs.

Examples of Protocol Alignment

BPC-157 10mg:

Common research cycles in published studies use 250mcg daily for 28 days (7mg total). Our 10mg vial supports the entire cycle in a single reconstitution, reducing handling errors and contamination risk compared to buying two 5mg vials.

TB-500 10mg:

Common research cycles in published studies use 5mg weekly for 2 weeks, then 2.5mg weekly (20-25mg total per cycle). Two 10mg vials eliminate the need for four 5mg vials—half the reconstitutions, half the waste.

CJC-1295 5mg & Ipamorelin 5mg:

We reverted these to 5mg after analyzing stability data. At 100mcg doses 3x/week, 5mg provides exactly 30 days of supply while maintaining peak potency. Larger 10mg vials risk degradation past week 4 post-reconstitution—protecting research quality matters more than appearing to offer "more."

NAD+ 1000mg:

Published longevity studies use 500-1000mg per administration. Our 1000mg vial eliminates the error-prone step of combining two 500mg vials for high-dose applications—one reconstitution, one draw, zero compounding mistakes.

This is what "boutique" means: Products engineered for how research is actually conducted, not just reselling Chinese factory standard sizes. Every size decision reflects clinical literature review and stability considerations.
