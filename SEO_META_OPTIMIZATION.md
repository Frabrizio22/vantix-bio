## Meta Optimization Principles

### Title Tag Formula (50-60 chars)
```
[Benefit/Hook] | [Keyword] | [Brand]
```

**Good examples:**
- "Purity ≠ Content: Read Janoshik COAs Like a Pro | Vantix Bio"
- "Semaglutide 10mg - Batch-Verified, $47 | Vantix Bio"

**Bad examples:**
- "How to Read a Janoshik COA | Vantix Bio" (boring)
- "Semaglutide 10mg | Vantix Bio" (no value prop)

### Meta Description Formula (150-155 chars)
```
[Problem] [Solution with numbers/specifics] [Unique differentiator]
```

**Good example:**
> "446 impressions but only 4 clicks? Your COA title is generic. Learn the purity vs content trap that costs researchers 23% potency. Batch-verified guide."

**Bad example:**
> "Learn how to read Janoshik COAs with this comprehensive guide."
