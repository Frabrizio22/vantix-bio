FAQ

Common questions about verification, ordering, and research peptides.

What makes Vantix different from other peptide vendors?+
Every batch is independently tested by Janoshik before release. We publish complete COAs (HPLC-DAD purity, LC-MS/MS identity, LAL endotoxin) for every product. QR codes on vials link directly to batch-specific results. No vendor claims, no trust-me testing — just third-party verification.
What is included in a Janoshik COA?+
Three independent tests per batch:

1. HPLC-DAD Purity — Quantitative analysis showing peptide concentration and impurity profile.
2. LC-MS/MS Identity — Molecular confirmation via mass spectrometry.
3. LAL Endotoxin — Bacterial contamination screening.

All results are publicly accessible via QR scan or batch lookup. No login required.
What is your purity threshold?+
All batches must meet ≥99% purity (HPLC-DAD), confirmed molecular identity (LC-MS/MS), and <0.05 EU/mg endotoxin (LAL). Batches that fail any test are rejected — no exceptions.
How do I verify my batch?+
Scan the QR code on your vial label or visit verify.html and enter your batch ID (e.g., VX-BPC10-001). You'll see the complete Janoshik COA including chromatograms, mass spectra, and endotoxin results.
