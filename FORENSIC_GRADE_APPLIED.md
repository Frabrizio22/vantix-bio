## Strategic Decision

After evaluating "Pharmaceutical-Grade" vs "Forensic-Grade" positioning:

**❌ REJECTED: "Pharmaceutical-Grade"**
- Regulatory risk: Triggers pharmacy/prescription filters for payment processors
- Conflicts with "Research Only" positioning
- Google/Meta ad platform auto-denies
- Already denied twice by Durango + PaymentCloud - can't afford more red flags

**✅ ADOPTED: "Forensic-Grade"**
- Stays in analytical science world (not medicine)
- Unique differentiation (no competitors use this)
- Matches Vantix Matrix infrastructure story
- Premium authority without regulatory risk
- Signals "verification infrastructure" not just "quality peptides"
- "Audit" = verifiable process vs "Grade" = subjective quality claim

## Final Hero Header

```
ISO-17025 Verified • Forensic-Grade Research Peptides
```

**Why this works:**
1. **"ISO-17025 Verified"** = proof (the credential)
2. **"Forensic-Grade"** = promise (the standard)
3. **"Research Peptides"** = product category
