💳 How to Pay Quarterly Taxes
Federal (IRS)
1. Go to irs.gov/payments
2. Select "Form 1040-ES"
3. Enter amount shown above
4. Pay with bank account (free)
5. Save confirmation number
California (FTB)
1. Go to ftb.ca.gov/pay
2. Select "Estimated Tax"
3. Enter 30% of federal amount
4. Pay with bank account
5. Save confirmation
💡 Tip: Total payment = Federal amount + (Federal × 0.3) for California. Dashboard shows combined estimate.
