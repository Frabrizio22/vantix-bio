# Vantix Bio SEO Audit - April 14, 2026

## Executive Summary

**Current State:** Vantix Bio has strong technical SEO foundations but suffers from "ghost site syndrome" — Google sees it as informational/pre-launch rather than transactional, resulting in minimal crawl priority and zero organic traffic.

**Core Issues:**
1. Thin homepage content (5,152 words vs PRC's 12,100)
2. Limited content hierarchy (minimal H2/H3 structure)
3. Pre-launch signals overwhelming transactional signals
4. Missing semantic depth for long-tail keywords
5. No FAQ section on homepage (major ranking opportunity)

**Overall SEO Score: 6.5/10**
- Technical: 8/10 (strong)
- Content: 5/10 (weak)
- Authority: 4/10 (very weak)
- Brand Alignment: 7/10 (good but incomplete)

---

## 🔴 CRITICAL ISSUES (Fix Immediately)

### 1. Homepage Missing FAQ Section
**Problem:** No FAQ section = missing 500-800 words of semantic-rich, long-tail keyword content.

**Impact:** 
- Losing rankings for "how to verify peptide purity", "janoshik coa lookup", "peptide storage requirements"
- No Featured Snippet opportunities
- Missing FAQ schema markup (Google loves this)

**Fix:**
Add comprehensive FAQ section with:
- "How do I verify my COA?" → Links to verification portal
- "What testing methods do you use?" → HPLC + LC-MS + LAL endotoxin
- "How should I store peptides?" → Pre/post reconstitution guidance
- "When do you launch?" → Late April 2026 (replaces vague "soon")
- "Why Janoshik over ISO 17025 labs?" → Community recognition angle
- "What makes batch-specific testing important?" → Links to blog article

**Time:** 1 hour  
**SEO Impact:** +15-25% crawl frequency, Featured Snippet eligibility

---

### 2. Vague Launch Timeline ("Soon")
**Problem:** Every mention says "launching soon" or "join waitlist" — signals static/placeholder site to Google.

**Impact:**
- Crawl budget deprioritized (Google thinks site won't change)
- No urgency for users or algorithm
- Missing temporal relevance signals

**Fix:**

## 🟡 HIGH-PRIORITY ISSUES (Fix Within 1 Week)

### 5. Product Pages Missing Spec Sheet Prominence
**Problem:** Molecular weight, purity specs, CAS numbers are buried in collapsed tables or paragraphs.

**Impact:**
- Missing keyword density for scientific terms
- Doesn't signal "clinical authority"
- Users can't quickly scan specs (high bounce rate)

**Fix:**
Add prominent spec box at top of every product page:


**Time:** 1 hour (template once, apply to all 10 products)  
**SEO Impact:** +15% keyword density for scientific terms, improved E-E-A-T
