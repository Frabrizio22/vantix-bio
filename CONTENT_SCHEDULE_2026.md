## FDA Compliance Rules for ALL Articles

### ✅ ALWAYS USE:
- "Research use only"
- "For laboratory research purposes"
- "In vitro / in vivo research applications"
- "Research protocols"
- "Laboratory preparation"
- "Metabolic research models"
- "Cellular signaling studies"
- "Preclinical research"

### ❌ NEVER USE:
- "Treatment" or "therapy"
- "Dosing" (use "research administration ranges")
- "Injection" (use "administration" or "in vivo delivery")
- "Weight loss" (use "metabolic regulation research")
- "Anti-aging" (use "aging research models")
- "Healing" (use "tissue repair models")
- Any health benefit claims
- Any human consumption language

### 🔒 STANDARD DISCLAIMER (Footer on every article):
```
FOR RESEARCH USE ONLY. All products are intended exclusively for laboratory 
research and are not intended for human consumption, diagnostic purposes, or 
therapeutic applications.
```
