## **Before:** Generic comparison article
## **After:** High-engagement conversion machine

## Title Optimization

**Before:** "Tirzepatide vs Retatrutide vs Semaglutide: What Actually Matters"

**After:** "Stop Wasting Money on the Wrong One"

**Why:** Pain point + urgency. SEO still works ("tirzepatide vs semaglutide") but adds emotional hook.

## Engagement Drivers Added

### 1. **Controversy/Hot Takes**
- "Most research should use tirzepatide" (controversial)
- "People buy sema because of herd mentality" (provocative)
- "Budget vendors love tirz because it's easier to fake" (insider info)

**Effect:** Drives comments, shares, arguments. Engagement signals to Google.

### 2. **Screenshot-Worthy Reference Card**
Grey box at bottom with all key info formatted for screenshots.

**Effect:** People screenshot and share on Reddit/Discord. Free marketing + backlinks.

### 3. **Internal Linking**
Links to batch COA article with teaser: "Spoiler: that COA might not be testing YOUR batch..."

**Effect:** Keeps readers on site longer (bounce rate ↓, session time ↑ = better SEO).

### 4. **Multiple CTAs Throughout**
- After each peptide section: Product link
- Mid-article: "Best value" callout
- End: Main shop CTA
- Internal: Next article teaser

**Effect:** More conversion opportunities = higher conversion rate.

### 5. **Cost Comparison Table With Study Math**
Added "100mg study cost" column showing real $$ impact.

**Effect:** Helps decision-making. More likely to buy when they see "$230 savings."
