What is Janoshik Analytical?

Janoshik is the most widely recognized independent analytical testing laboratory in the research peptide and performance compound space. Based in the Czech Republic, they've built a reputation over years of consistent, transparent, publicly verifiable results.

Their name carries weight because the community has verified their work thousands of times over. When a vendor publishes a Janoshik COA, it's not just a PDF — it's a report that anyone can independently verify through Janoshik's own public results portal.

Key Point: Janoshik is the closest thing the peptide community has to an industry-standard testing authority. Their Task ID system allows anyone to verify that a COA is real — not fabricated by a vendor.

Why Janoshik Matters for Peptide Research

The research peptide industry has a verification problem: most vendors accept supplier certificates without independent testing. This creates systematic risk:

  • Mislabeled products — compound doesn't match label claim
  • Incorrect concentrations — under or over-dosed vials
  • Contamination — bacterial endotoxins, solvent residues
  • Fabricated COAs — vendors generating fake certificates

Janoshik's independent, publicly verifiable testing eliminates these risks. You don't have to trust the vendor's word — you can check the data yourself.

What Janoshik Tests

Janoshik offers a comprehensive analytical panel for peptides:

  • HPLC purity analysis — quantifies how pure the compound is
  • Mass spectrometry (MS) identity — confirms the molecular identity matches the label
  • Endotoxin testing (LAL) — screens for bacterial contamination
  • Sterility testing — verifies absence of microbial growth

This multi-method approach means every batch is verified for both what it is and how clean it is.

How Vantix Bio Uses Janoshik

Every Vantix Bio batch is sent to Janoshik for independent testing before release. We test with complementary methods:

  • HPLC-DAD (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography with Diode Array Detection) — quantifies purity
  • LC-MS/MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) — confirms molecular identity
  • LAL endotoxin testing — screens for bacterial contamination

We publish full Certificates of Analysis on our batch verification portal. Every vial can be traced to its specific Janoshik analytical report — and you can cross-verify directly on Janoshik's own portal.

Our Standard: ≥98% purity verified by complementary methods. If a batch doesn't meet this threshold, it doesn't ship.

Janoshik vs. Other Testing Claims

When evaluating peptide vendors, understand the difference:

  • "Tested" — could mean anything, no standard
  • "COA provided" — certificate could be from the supplier (not independent)
  • "Third-party tested" — better, but which lab? Can you verify it?
  • "Janoshik-verified" — independent lab, publicly verifiable, community-trusted ✓

The difference is verifiability. Anyone can generate a PDF. Janoshik results can be independently confirmed through their Task ID system.
