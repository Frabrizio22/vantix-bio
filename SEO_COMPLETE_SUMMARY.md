## Meta Optimization Formula Applied

### Title Tag Strategy
**Formula:** [Insight/Hook] | [Keyword] | [Price/Benefit] | Brand

**Examples:**
- "Purity ≠ Content: Why Your Janoshik COA Has 2 Numbers That Matter"
- "Sema 14.9%, Tirz 20.9%, Reta 24.2%: GLP-1 Comparison + Purity Trap"
- "Tirzepatide 30mg - Batch-Verified $68 | SURMOUNT-5 Head-to-Head Winner"

**Why it works:**
- Leads with surprising insight (hooks attention)
- Includes primary keyword (SEO)
- Adds quantified benefit or price (value prop)
- Brand at end (trust signal)

---

### Meta Description Strategy
**Formula:** [Problem] [Solution with numbers/specifics] [Unique differentiator]

**Examples:**
- "98% pure but 75% content = your peptide is 23% weaker than you calculated. Learn to spot the difference on Janoshik COAs and avoid paying for filler. Batch-verified guide with examples."
- "Endotoxins trigger TLR4 inflammation even at <5 EU/mg, confounding preclinical data. Why HPLC purity isn't enough and what LAL assays actually measure."

**Why it works:**
- Identifies clear problem (relatable)
- Quantifies impact (23% weaker, <5 EU/mg)
- Promises specific solution (avoid paying for filler)
- Differentiates (batch-verified, LAL assays)
