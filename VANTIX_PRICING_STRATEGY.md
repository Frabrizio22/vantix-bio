## PRICING PSYCHOLOGY

### Price Points That Work:
- **$24, $28, $30, $34, $38, $42, $48** (even numbers, clean)
- Avoid: $31, $37, $43 (feels arbitrary)
- Psychological barrier: **$50** (stay under when possible)

### Bundle Perception:
- 10% off = minimal motivation
- **12-15% off = strong motivation**
- 20%+ off = erodes perceived value

### Seasonal Pricing:
- MT-2: Can push to $32-34 in April-June (peak demand)
- GLP: Stable year-round (January surge, but consistent)
