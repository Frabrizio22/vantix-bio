Frequently Asked Questions

Transparency extends to how we communicate.

Every Vantix batch is sent to Janoshik Analytical for independent third-party testing using HPLC-DAD (purity) and LC-MS/MS (identity). Janoshik is a globally recognized analytical laboratory specializing in peptide and pharmaceutical chromatography.

Each compound receives dual chromatographic analysis. The Verification Portal displays both HPLC-DAD and LC-MS/MS reports side by side with forensic watermarking, providing a tamper-evident audit trail that cannot be fabricated or reused from another source.

A unique alphanumeric identifier assigned to every batch. Enter it in the Verification Portal to instantly retrieve the full analytical report, including purity percentage, identity confirmation, and the original Janoshik reference number.

HPLC-DAD measures purity but cannot definitively confirm molecular identity. LC-MS/MS provides mass spectrometric confirmation of the exact compound. Together, they eliminate both contamination and substitution risks — the forensic standard for analytical verification.
