Last updated: June 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vantix Bio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you visit or make a purchase from vantixbio.com (the "Site"). We are committed to handling your information responsibly.

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • To process, fulfill, and ship your orders and respond to inquiries.

  • To communicate with you about orders, waitlist status, or updates you have requested.
  • To measure and improve marketing, including through advertising pixels, where enabled.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security, or compliance issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Information

We share personal information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the business — payment processors, shipping carriers, hosting providers, and analytics or advertising platforms — under obligations to protect the information.
  • Legal and safety recipients where required by law, to enforce our Terms, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

We do not sell your personal information for money. To the extent that the use of advertising technologies such as the Meta Pixel is considered "sharing" or "selling" under California law, you may opt out as described below.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Site, remember your preferences (including age/research-use acknowledgment), measure traffic, and support advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect Site functionality.

5. Your Privacy Rights (California Residents)

Depending on applicable law, California residents may have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use and share it.
  • Request access to or a copy of their personal information.
  • Request deletion of their personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@vantixbio.com. We will verify your request and respond as required by applicable law. You may also use an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf.

6. Data Retention and Security

We retain personal information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described here, including order, tax, and recordkeeping obligations, and then delete or de-identify it. We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children

The Site is intended only for persons 21 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.
