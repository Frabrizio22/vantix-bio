## Visual Design Principles Applied

### 1. Progressive Disclosure
- TL;DR first (30 sec)
- Key Takeaways per section (visual pop)
- Full technical details for those who want depth
- Quick Decision Guide at end (fast conclusion)

### 2. Color Psychology
- **Blue (Semaglutide):** Trust, established, clinical
- **Yellow (Tirzepatide):** Innovation, energy, dual-action
- **Pink (Retatrutide):** Cutting-edge, experimental, advanced
- **Red:** Warnings, critical information

### 3. F-Pattern Scanning
- Left-aligned key points
- Bold headers at left edge
- Callout boxes break up text walls
- Gradient backgrounds attract eye

### 4. Information Hierarchy
```
Level 1: TL;DR (Everyone reads)
Level 2: Key Takeaway boxes (Skim readers read)
Level 3: Section headers (Scan for relevance)
Level 4: Body paragraphs (Technical readers)
Level 5: Spec boxes (Deep dive)
```

**Result:** Readers choose their depth level naturally
