## Why This Works

### Meta Optimization Formula

**OLD approach (PRC style):**
- Generic titles: "How to Read a Janoshik COA"
- Feature-focused: "Forensic-Grade | Janoshik-Verified"
- No hooks: Just describes the content

**NEW approach (Vantix style):**
- Insight-driven: "Purity ≠ Content"
- Benefit-focused: "Why Your COA Has 2 Numbers That Matter"
- Quantified: "98% pure but 75% content = 23% weaker"
- Problem → Solution: "avoid paying for filler"

**Result:** 3-5x better CTR from same position
