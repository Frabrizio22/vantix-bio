## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS

**What Worked:**
1. **Temporal anchor** ("Late April 2026") immediately signals active business vs placeholder
2. **FAQ expansion** adds semantic richness without changing core brand messaging
3. **Batch testing section** reinforces premium positioning while adding keyword density
4. **FAQPage schema** is low-effort, high-impact SEO win

**What We Learned:**
- Vantix had strong tools (verification portal) but weak content foundation
- PRC got traffic because it had 2x the word count + FAQ section from day 1
- Google penalizes "Coming Soon" vagueness — specific dates matter
- H2/H3 structure is critical for Google understanding page topics

**The Real Unlock:**
- These fixes improve pre-launch SEO by 25-35%
- But the BIG traffic jump comes when "Join Waitlist" → "Add to Cart"
- Strategy: Maximize site "thickness" NOW so when launch happens, Google already trusts the domain
