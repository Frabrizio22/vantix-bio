## What "Verified" Looks Like

✅ **Good verification:**
- Multiple sources (medical news, FDA, trial registry) confirm the number
- Primary source (NEJM article abstract) visible even behind paywall
- Wikipedia summary matches your claim

❌ **Red flags:**
- No sources found for specific number
- Sources contradict your claim
- Only one obscure blog mentions it

---

## Safety Net: Conservative Framing

If you can't verify a specific number, use these alternatives:

**Instead of:** "20.9% weight reduction"  
**Use:** "approximately 20% weight reduction" OR "up to 21% in clinical trials"

**Instead of:** "24.2% at 12mg dose"  
**Use:** "over 20% weight reduction at higher doses in Phase 2 trials"

**Instead of:** "superior hunger control"  
**Use:** "dual receptor activation may contribute to appetite regulation through complementary pathways"
