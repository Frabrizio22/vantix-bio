## Clinical Trial Data Verification

### Semaglutide
**Trial Program:** STEP (Semaglutide Treatment Effect in People with obesity)
- **Primary Reference:** STEP 1 (NEJM 2021) ✅ Current
- **Weight Reduction:** 14.9% at 68 weeks (2.4 mg weekly)
- **Participants:** 1,961 subjects, 68-week study
- **Status:** Post-market, established safety database (>7 years)
- **Other Trials:** STEP 2-8, SUSTAIN, SELECT - all still relevant

**Research Currency:** ✅ EXCELLENT
- Most comprehensive GLP-1 dataset available
- 2021 publication still standard reference
- Ongoing post-market surveillance adds safety data

---

### Tirzepatide
**Trial Program:** SURMOUNT (obesity), SURPASS (diabetes)
- **Primary Reference:** SURMOUNT-1 (NEJM 2022) ✅ Current
- **Weight Reduction:** 20.9% at 72 weeks (15 mg weekly)
- **Participants:** 2,539 subjects, 72-week study
- **Head-to-Head:** SURMOUNT-5 (NEJM 2025) ✅ Very Recent
  - Direct comparison vs semaglutide
  - Confirmed superiority in weight reduction
- **Status:** Recently approved, actively studied

**Research Currency:** ✅ EXCELLENT
- 2022 primary data still current
- 2025 head-to-head data is cutting-edge
- Most recent large-scale GIP/GLP-1 dual agonist data

---

### Retatrutide
**Trial Program:** Phase 2 (NEJM 2023), TRIUMPH (Phase 3 ongoing)
- **Primary Reference:** Phase 2 trial (NEJM 2023, Jastreboff et al.) ✅ Current
- **Weight Reduction:** 24.2% at 48 weeks (12 mg weekly)
- **Participants:** 338 subjects, 48-week dose-ranging study
- **Phase 3:** TRIUMPH-1, -2, -3 currently enrolling
- **Expected Data:** 2025-2026
- **Status:** Investigational, NOT FDA approved

**Research Currency:** ✅ CURRENT (with caveats)
- Latest available data (2023)
- Phase 3 ongoing - more data coming
- Mechanism less characterized than sema/tirz
- Article appropriately notes limited data
